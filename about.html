About BitcoinWeather

BitcoinWeather turns complex statistical forecasts into a clear and friendly design. Like a weather app, you can instantly see where Bitcoin might head in the next days and months.

Introduction

BitcoinWeather makes Bitcoin forecasts easy to understand for everyone. With its simple design you can view the forecast up to 360 days ahead without needing to study charts or technical analysis.

Our Vision

We believe that Bitcoin analysis should be accessible and transparent. Our innovative interface makes it incredibly fast and efficient to learn about future price developments. And the best part: BitcoinWeather is and will remain completely free of charge.

Innovation

Forget conventional chart overload. BitcoinWeather combines solid statistical methods with the familiar style of a weather forecast. The result: a modern, elegant way to see the outlook for Bitcoin at a glance.
