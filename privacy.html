Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how BitcoinWeather collects, uses and protects information.

Data Collection

We do not require personal data to use BitcoinWeather. Only when you use the contact form your name, email and message will be transmitted by email to us.

Cookies

BitcoinWeather itself does not set tracking cookies. However, third-party services (like embedded fonts or APIs) may set technical cookies.

Use of Data

Your contact details will be used only to reply to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent or share your information with third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, but no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please do not send highly sensitive data via the contact form.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date.
